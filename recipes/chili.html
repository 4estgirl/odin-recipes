Chili

Description

This delicious chili is very easy to make, and extremely adaptible. The recipe below calls for frozen corn, but I've also substituted green beans, bell peppers, and zucchini. Feel free to experiment with adding extra vegetables!

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • 1-2lbs ground meat
  • 1 large yellow onion
  • 3-4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 quart bag frozen corn
  • 1 16 ounce can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 8 ounce can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cans red kidney beans, black beans, or chili beans
  • 1/2 cup uncooked elbow noodles
  • coriander powder
  • cumin powder
  • cayenne powder

Steps

  1. If frozen, thaw ground meat.
  2. Over medium high heat, heat a drizzle of oil in a large pot.
  3. Dice onion into 1/2 inch pieces. Mince garlic.
  4. Combine ground meat, onion, and garlic in pot. Cook until meat is browned, and onions are translucent, stirring frequently to prevent burning.
  5. While meat cooks, cook elbow noodles in separate pot until al dente.
  6. Add frozen corn to browned meat. Stir.
  7. Add tomatoes and beans to meat mixture. Stir. Reduce heat to medium low when the mixture begins to simmer.
  8. Drain noodles, then add to chili.
  9. Season with coriander, cumin, and cayenne to taste.
  10. Serve with shredded cheese and crackers.
